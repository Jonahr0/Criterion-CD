About SDG 6.1
SDG 6.1 is " By 2030, achieve universal and equitable access to safe and affordable drinking water for all." This essentially means to get everyone regardless of age,race,origin, and gender will have a reasonably easy access to safe and affordable drinking water sources. In countries like Canada and America we take water for granted and we don’t realize how important it is to daily life. Some people around the world don’t have easy access to clean water and spend their whole day getting water and boiling it to remove the bacteria just to drink or be able to cook their food.
